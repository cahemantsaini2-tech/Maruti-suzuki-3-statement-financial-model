# Maruti Suzuki FP&A Financial Model
## 📌 Overview A dynamic, 3-statement financial forecasting model of Maruti Suzuki India Limited projecting performance through FY30. This project demonstrates advanced FP&A capabilities, moving beyond static historical reporting to provide forward-looking, driver-based analysis. 
## 🚀 Core Features 
**1. Driver-Based Revenue Build:** Top-line growth strictly modeled on operational drivers (Domestic vs. Export, Volume vs. Average Selling Price). 

**2. Dynamic Scenario Manager:** Integrated control panel to instantly stress-test financials across **Base, Upside, and Downside** scenarios. 

**3. Working Capital Sensitivity:** Cash flow and liquidity accurately driven by operational days (Receivables, Inventory, Payables). 

**4. FP&A Best Practices:** Clean 3-statement linkage (P&L, WC, CFS) with zero hardcoded plugs in forecasted years and standard color-coding. 

**5. Executive Dashboard:** Visual summary of critical KPIs including Revenue CAGR, EBITDA margins, and PAT trajectories.
